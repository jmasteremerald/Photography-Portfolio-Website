Who am I?

My name is Jazz Patterson, and I am a photographer based in South Florida. I’m quite a proponent of the arts in general. Aside from being a photographer, I’m also a stage and screen actor, writer, filmmaker, and tennis athlete. My passion is telling stories in new ways and about new things. Each new person I get to work, I take as an opportunity to learn and make a new experience. It’s my pleasure to work with you and create the high quality images you deserve.
